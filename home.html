About Teacher Leah

Leah has a Bachelor's Degree in Jewish Education and a Master's Degree in Social Work. She has experience teaching Hebrew in after school programs, but mainly tutors privately for groups and individuals. Due to the Coronavirus, she is teaching remotely via Zoom. Depending on the level, she teaches the language through Hebrew literature, newspaper articles, and Israeli culture. For a tailored Hebrew learning experience, learn with Leah!
